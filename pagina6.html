Pregunta 22: What is a TRUE statement in reporting pressure ulcers?

c. Two codes are assigned when a patient is admitted with a pressure ulcer that evolves to another stage during the admission.
d. The site of the ulcer and the stage of the ulcer are reported with two separate codes.
